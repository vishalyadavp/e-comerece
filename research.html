Ayurveda and Wellness

Ayurveda is an ancient healing system originating in South Asia that draws inspiration from the Sanskrit words ayus (life) and veda (science). The science and practice of Ayurveda are narrated in ancient texts such as Charaka Samhita, a treatise on general medicine written in the Indus Valley area around 1000 BC in Sanskrit. According to Ayurvedic texts, the human body comprises three body states: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha. When the three body states are in perfect harmony, the individual enjoys good health, whereas an imbalance causes diseases. Ayurveda seeks to address this state of imbalance through a process of holistic healing that includes natural and herbal treatments, wellness practices such as diet modifications and lifestyle changes, and exercise and meditation.
